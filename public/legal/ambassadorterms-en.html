Introduction

These Terms and Conditions (hereinafter also simply as "terms" or "conditions") for Ambassadors (hereinafter also as "Ambassadors" or "referrer") shall manage sebrussi.com referral program.

Our referral program consists of encouraging people to tell other people about our website, products or services.

You must not use this website referral program, if you disagree with any of these website Terms and Conditions.


Ambassador

With the term "Ambassador" we define a person who is registered on our web site and has been appointed to act as referrer.

Ambassadors will solely and uniquely act as referrer and as such he/she should not act as representative or in name or behalf of sebrussi.com.

La concessione di agire come Ambassador non costituisce in alcun modo alcuna forma di accordo o intesa vincolante su sebrussi.com o su qualsiasi società collegata, espressa o implicita, che sebrussi.com o qualsiasi sua società collegata assumerà o impegnera' un Ambassador per un certo periodo di tempo, né interferirà in alcun modo con il diritto di sebrussi.com di revocare la posizione di Ambassador o i nostri referral programs in qualsiasi momento e senza preavviso, il cui diritto è riservato.

As Ambassador:

  • once you are approved as ambassador, you must initially purchase 3 products (with 20% discount) from sebrussi.com website in order to be appointed as Ambassador.
  • you will be given a Promo Code;
  • you may recommend sebrussi.com website to other people;
  • you may refer and post sebrussi.com link and content in your social media accounts;
  • you may give your Promo Code to other people in order to allow them to get discounted purchases;
  • you may benefit of special discounts on your personal sebrussi.com purchases;
  • you will receive referral fees on sebrussi.com purchases made by people using your Promo Code;

Discounts and Fees

Discounts and referral fees percentages are not negotiable and may be subject to change at any time (check periodically this page for any update):

  • a 10% discount is granted to people buying products with promo codes;
  • a 10% referral fee is granted to Ambassadors on products purchased with their Promo Code;
  • a 20% discount is granted to an Ambassador for his/her own purchases;

Referral Fees

Ambassadors shall exchange some administrative info in order to process billing and payments of referral fees.

On a monthly basis, Ambassadors will receive a document reporting the overall monthly referral fees and the monthly payment made by sebrussi.com.

Payment will be always processed in EUR and may be processed by bank transfers (bank charges will be shared) or may be processed by PayPal (in this case Ambassador should have a PayPal account).

In order to minimize transfers charges impact on referral fees (especially bank charges, which are completely out of our control), Ambassadors may notify sebrussi.com to be paid on a quaterly basis or when referral fees will reach a certain threshold.

Ambassadors may also notify sebrussi.com to be paid by using his/her earnings as additional discount on his/her own next purchases.
